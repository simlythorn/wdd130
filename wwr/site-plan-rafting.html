Overview

Purpose

We want to attract many customers to purchase Whitewater Rafting Tours so we can have a successful business. However, the client is not greedy. She wants the customers to get a good value. So,this website is more about providing a valuable service for a fair price, not about trying to sell the most expensive tour to every customer.

We want to provide the right tour to each client. So we will have packages for different clients. We will have a Family Package, Couple's Package, Friends's Package, Individual Package, and Reunion Package (for large extended family). We also want to provide training for people who want to improve their Whitewater Rafting skills.

We can also provide food and transportation and make sure the clients have an enjoyable time through a good environment. We provide a good meal by the river before and after each whitewater rafting trip.

Audience

Individuals, couples, friends, and families who are interested in an adventure for a fair price. This adventure is for everyone, no matter who you are and where you are from, but kids are allowed for the family trips only. This tour is especailly for those who are stressed in their daily lives from studying or working, so that it will help refresh their minds and rejuvenate their bodies, so they can continuce their work again afterwards. Moreover, for someone who is a single person, they still can do this tour because we offer them the opportunity to join other people who are single to save money and make new friends.
